WHATEVER YOU WANT COOKIES


  • Time: 1 Hour
  • Serving size 1 Cookie
  • Serves: 20
Ingredients Amount
Butter Softend Two Sticks/8 Ounces
Sugar 1 Cup
Brown SUGAr 1&1/4 TO 1&1/4 CUpS
EGG 2 BABY CHICKENS
VANILLA LIKE A TEASPOON
FLOUR LIKE 3 OR 4 CUPS ROUGHLY
MIX INS WHATEVER THE F YOU WANT

Step 1: PUT BUTTER AND SUGAR IN BOWL MIX THAT SHIT TILL CREAMY

STEP 2:PUT EGGS AND VANILLA IN ONE AT A TIME KEEPING MIXING

STEP 3:ADD FLOUR SLOWLY TILL ITS A THICK DOUGH SHOULD BE MOULDABLE AND BE ABLE TO BE PICKED UP WITH YOUR HANDS

STEP 4:ADD WHATEVER YOU WANT TO THE COOKIES THEY WILL BAKE WITH ANY MIX INS YOU WANT

STEP 5:BAKE AT 350 FOR LIKE 18 to 20 Minutes!

STEP 6: LET COOL FOR A LITTLE BIT THEN EAT

Lemon Shortbread Cookies


  • Time 4 Hours
  • Serving Size 2 Cookies
  • Serves: 20
Ingredients Amount
SUGA 2/3 CUP OR 1 CUP IDK
ZEST OF lemon 2 SPOONTABLES
BUTTER 1 Cup
LEMON JUICE 1 to 2 SPOONS OF TABLES
All PURPOSE FLOUT 2 CUPS
CORNSTARCH 1 to 2/3 Cups
SALT LIKE A PINCH IDK SALTY
VANILLA EXTRACT 1/2 TEASPOON

Step 1: MIX LEMON ZEST AND SUGAR TOGETHER IN A FOOD PROCESSOR THEN MIX TILL HOMOGENEOUS

STEP 2:COMBINE LEMON SUGAR AND BUTTER TILL CREAMY (SCRAPE AS NEEDED)

STEP 3:Add Lemon Juice and Vanilla

STEP 4:ADD FLOUR, CORN STARCH AND SALT Till dough DOUGHS

STEP 5:Roll dough out on parchment or counter with flour till 1/2 to 1/4 of an inch. Cover and Refriderate for 2 to 3 hours

STEP 6:CUT OUT COOKIES WITH COOKIE CUTTER REPEAT TILL NO MORE DOUGH

STEP 7:BAKE AT 350 FOR 15 to 16 MINUTES TILL LIGHT BROWN

Let Cool And Enjoy

Snickerdoodle Cookies


  • Time 1 Hour
  • Serving Size 1 Cookies
  • Serves: 24
Ingredients Amount
SUGA 1 1/2 CUPS
CHICKEN BABYS 2
BUTTER 1 Cup
CREAM OF TARTAR 2 TEASPOONS
All PURPOSE FLOUT 2 2/3 CUPS
Baking SODA 1 TEASPOON
Sugar 2nd time 3 Tablespoons
CINNAMONOM 3 Spoon of tea
VANILLA EXTRACT Teaspoon one
SALT 1/4 teaspoon

Step 1: MIX SUGAR AND BUTTER TILL CREAMY

STEP 2:ADD EGGS AND VANILLA MIX WELL

STEP 3:ADD FLOUR, CREAM OF TARTAR, BAKING SODA, AND SALT MIX TILL DOUGH FORMS

STEP 4:MIX SUGAR AND CINNAMON IN A SMALL BOWL

STEP 5:ROLL DOUGH INTO 1 Inch BALLS THEN ROLL IN SUGAR CINNAMON MIXTURE PLACE ON UNGREASED COOKIE SHEET

STEP 6:BAKE AT 350 FOR 10 to 12 MINUTES TILL LIGHT BROWN AROUND EDGES

REMOVE FROM PAN IMMEDIETLY

LET COOL AND ENJOY
